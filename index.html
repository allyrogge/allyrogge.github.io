What I'm All About

Art and Writing

My friends may know all too well about my penchant for Spotify playlists and videos, but writing is something I haven't engaged in for a while. On my way to South Africa last week, I found myself compelled to pick up a pen again. Combined with my newly forged computer coding skills, this website was born!

Making Music

As you can read about in my blog below, I've found great importance in music: creating art and sharing it has shaped me since age 4. From Croatia to St. Paul's Cathedral in the Vatican, I've learned just how universal the joy of music is.

Exploration

Nothing is more important than the ability to know the world and those that occupy it. That can best (and perhaps, only) be achieved when we can empathize with different types of people. From Europe to my current adventures in South Africa, I've gotten to learn about many types of people, and can only hope to be able to continue to do so, whether in my own community or abroad.

Family/Friends

Raised by some of the best Midwesterners around, I am a believer in the power of faith, family, and good ole' fashioned frienship. The power of a strong, encouraging community continues to drive me in all that I do.
